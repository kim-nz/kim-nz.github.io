It's Me! Kim Anand

Kia Ora! My name is Kim, I grew up in Tamaki Makaurau (Auckland).

I have also lived in Melbourne and Toronto for extended periods of time and consider both cities my homes away from home.

I used to work in the Shipping industry but mid-pandemic I decided it was time for a change.

Reading is one of my favorite pass-times and you can usually find me in a sunny corner of the house with a cup of coffee and a book.

I'm also about to start learning how to make pottery. I was surprised by how popular pottery classes are - the class I'm enrolled in sold out in less than 3 minutes!

Apparently I've decided 2022 is the year to really stretch myself because I've also taken up GOLF. GOLF! My dad was an avid golfer back in his day and really wanted me and my sister to learn when we were younger - we had no interest. However, a few of my friends in Toronto played, and we ended up spending quite a bit of time at driving ranges. Yo. Golf is HARD. It's endlessly technical and keeping all the elements of form in mind while you try (TRY) to hit a golfball ball is TOUGH - and incredibly satisfying when you pull it off.
